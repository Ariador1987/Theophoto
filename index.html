About

 

I grew up in Split, Croatia and now I call the city of Kaštela my home. I'm an all around photographer who mainly does lifestyle photography, weddings, baptisms and such.

Where there's a moment to be captured my camera is ready, so far it has been a wild ride and I've been loving every second of it.
I appreciate the spontaneity of the moment and the fact I'm the one behind the lens to capture it.

I hope you'll join me on my journey to our mutual satisfaction.
